### Motion — list item remove exit

From find-animation-opportunities (highest leverage): list detail **Remove** no longer teleports.

- `lists/[id]/page.tsx`: exit via opacity + `translateX(8px)` + `grid-rows` collapse (~200ms ease-out).
- Persist `removeFromList` immediately; **delay `refresh()`** until exit finishes so batch reload doesn’t yank the row.
- `prefers-reduced-motion`: opacity only, 120ms.
- Item count / total ignore exiting rows during the animation.
